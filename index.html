Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zero GPT Detector ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our AI text detection service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Text submissions: Text you submit for analysis is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI text detection analysis
  • Improve our detection algorithms
  • Send newsletter updates (if subscribed)
  • Respond to your inquiries

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Text submissions are processed in real-time and are not stored or used for any purpose other than providing immediate analysis results.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service operates through ChatGPT. When you use Zero GPT Detector, you are also subject to OpenAI's terms of service and privacy policy.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences (such as cookie consent). You can control cookie settings through your browser.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of newsletter communications
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@zerochatgpt.pro.
